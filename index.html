Our View

The Scarlet Skein is more than just a website for me. It's an evolution of my passion of teaching and crocheting. It's been a rollercoaster of a journey with lots of twists and turns. It's been a huge learning curve as I navigated my way around growing a business from scratch.

Inspiration Starts Here

If you’re like us, stitching is more than just a hobby. It’s a way of life! With a skein of yarn and some simple tools, you create more than socks, blankets, or hats; you create beloved family heirlooms that will be passed from generation to generation. You take part in traditions that have had a place in human culture for hundreds of years. These are traditions you can continue to share with younger generations of makers.
